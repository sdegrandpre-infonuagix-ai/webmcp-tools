The Roasting Philosophy

Our Classic Dark Roast isn't just about intensity; it's about the nuance within the darkness. We aim for the "Second Crack"—the precise moment when the beans release their internal pressure and oils begin to surface.

The result is a heavy-bodied cup with zero acidity, replaced instead by a smooth, lingering sweetness that evokes high-quality dark chocolate.
